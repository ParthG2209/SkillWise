# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We, as contributors and maintainers of **SkillWise**, pledge to provide a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free environment for everyone involved in this project.

SkillWise is built to support students and self-learners in their skill development journey, and we believe a positive and inclusive community is essential to that mission.

---

## Our Standards

### Expected behavior
Participants are expected to:
- Use respectful, inclusive, and professional language
- Be considerate of different perspectives and learning backgrounds
- Provide constructive feedback and helpful suggestions
- Accept criticism gracefully and focus on improvement
- Collaborate in good faith to enhance the project

### Unacceptable behavior
Unacceptable behavior includes, but is not limited to:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private or sensitive information without consent
- Abusive, threatening, or intimidating behavior
- Any conduct that disrupts collaboration or creates an unsafe environment

---

## Maintainer Responsibilities
Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Reviewing issues, pull requests, and discussions respectfully
- Taking appropriate action in response to violations
- Ensuring the project remains welcoming and constructive

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

---

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces, including GitHub repositories, issues, and pull requests
- All interactions related to SkillWise, whether online or offline

---

## Enforcement
If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to the project maintainers.

Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with confidentiality.  
Actions may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent removal from the project, depending on the severity of the violation.
